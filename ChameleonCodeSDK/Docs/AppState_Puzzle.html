Treasure Hunt Back Story: The last will and testament of Arthur A. Reality names you and four others as a potential heir, if you prove yourself worthy, to a fortune of $5,000. To prove yourself, you must travel to Madison, Wisconsin, by November 19 and compete in a contest. The contest will test your wits, and your character, through a series of five puzzles. The answers may be right under your nose. To stay in the game, you will need to support your favorite businesses with a purchase of $1 or more. With each separate purchase, you will earn a life. Those lives will come in handy as you play through the puzzles, so don’t let them fall below zero, or you’ll have to start over.

These are the puzzles for a sample hunt (json) included in the SDK.

Puzzle 1 - One Barrel

The Story: Arthur A. Reality lived in the Atwood Neighborhood in east Madison. No promises, but it might be a good idea to start your hunt there. I hope you're familiar with Wisconsin traditions!

A possible hint: Seventh inning stretch/ Sing it loud in Milwaukee/ What's four minus three?

After figuring out the puzzle, and verifying that they are at 'One Barrel', they have to answer a question.

The Question: What Swiss meat is sold here? (Spell it right!)

Answer: Landjäger

Puzzle 2 - Stone Fence

The Story: Good job! Mr. Reality would be proud. This next test might require a thesaurus. You do carry one with you always, right?

A possible hint: Rock me mama like/ another brick in the wall/ Synonyms are friends

After figuring out the puzzle, and verifying that they are at 'Stone Fence', they have to answer a question.

The Question: How many yellow windows are on the gray building in the window?

Answer: 6

Puzzle 3 - Barrymore Theater

The Story: Steady as she goes, you are almost halfway there! The Princess Bride was Mr. Reality's favorite movie. If you got tired of his pitiful poetry, and told him, "No more rhyming I mean it!" He'd answer, "Anybody want a peanut?"

A possible hint: Bury your strife and/ Marry for life and make/ No more awful rhymes

After figuring out the puzzle, and verifying that they are at 'Barrymore Theater', they have to answer a question.

The Question: What does BDF stand for?

Answer: Bad Dog Frida

Puzzle 4 - The Mermaid Cafe

The Story: Nice work! Did you know how old Mr. Reality was when he died? He was 104! What's his secret? Coffee, coffee, and more coffee.

A possible hint: Roosters are known to/ Go swimming under the sea/ When there is coffee

After figuring out the puzzle, and verifying that they are at 'The Mermaid Cafe', they have to answer a question.

A possible hint: Medieval lore says/ Drink this and live forever/ It's pseudo science
